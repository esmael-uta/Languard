About Langaurd

Languard is an AI-powered platform dedicated to preserving endangered languages, particularly the Orang Asli languages of Malaysia. Our mission is to prevent language extinction by creating digital resources, educational tools, and AI systems that keep these languages alive and accessible.

Using Amazon Web Services and advanced machine learning, we're building a comprehensive ecosystem for language preservation that benefits both current speakers and future generations.
